About Us

Evolve Business Excellence specializes in facilities and maintenance management, focusing on property, people, technology and business infrastructure consultancy and operational support services. We align our facilities and maintenance management consultancy services both in the UK and Globally to your business and property needs, goals and objectives. Our Facilities and Maintenance Management consultants in the UK and Globally deliver successful solutions to organizations in both the private and public sectors. Our overarching objectives are to ensure: Value - We always ready to deliver and ensure that you experience 'Best Value' outcomes Performance - Ensure optimum structures, resources and organizational performance Risk - Transfer, mitigate and manage your risks

Our Services

Our facility management and maintenance consultancy and support services focus on: business transformation, cost optimisation, increased service quality and performance, working efficiencies and improvement; delivered professionally across the following areas:

  • Strategic alignment of the FM function within the core business
  • Organizational reviews for optimum positioning of the FM service
  • Quality-Value index (QVi) process application for business improvement and business transformation
  • Development of operational and occupational budgets and cost plans
  • Cost reduction and cost recovery services
  • Facilities management sourcing options, facilities procurement & tendering services in the UK
  • Mobilization management support services
  • Flexible contract support services and interim facilities management services
  • Facilities and Maintenance Management audits for compliance and improvement
  • Expert witness & adjudication services
  • Bespoke services such as benchmarking, development of KPI models, SLA preparation etc.
  • Workshops & training
  • FM Navigator – FM Project Management application

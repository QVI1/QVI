The Sims 4 Cool Kitchen Stuff Pack

The Sims 4 Cool Kitchen is the third stuff pack that has been released for The Sims 4; the pack contains a variety of new objects to style your sims perfect kitchen and dining area. An ice cream machine has been added to satisfy your sims sweet tooth which can either give positive or negative Moodlets.

Meal Moodlets

  • Pure Perfection Moodlet gives Happy+1 for four hours
  • Weight of the World Moodlet gives Happy +1 for four hours but can make your sim gain weight
  • Taste of Diet Moodlet gives Happy+2 for four hours and can reduce the weight of sims if eaten enough
  • Twin Roses Moodlet gives Flirty+1 for four hours
  • Haunted Mint gives 2 different effects and gives the chance of a ghost visiting your sim
  • Hauntingly Saddened Moodlet gives Sad+` for four hours
  • Hauntingly Inspired Moodlet gives Inspiried +1 for four hours
  • Mental Upheaval Moodlet gives a random moodlet +1 for one hour
  • Plant Matter Moodlet gives Happy+1 for four hours and turns your sim green
  • Dragons Breath Moodlet gives Uncomfortable moodlet+1 for four hours, Sims have a chance of being set on fire
  • Chilling Mocha moodlet gives 2 different effects and can result in your sim having a special freeze interaction
  • Chilled to the Bone Moodlet gives Uncomfortable+1 for 4 hours, also turns your sims skin blue
  • Ice Treat Moodlet gives Happy+1 for four hours.
